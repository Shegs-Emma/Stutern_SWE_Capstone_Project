Naruto can be described with the following three words:

Friend

A tailed beast is a creature of immese power, capable of destroying a country in a slow afternoon. There are 9 of them, ranging from a 1-tail, to the 9-tails. The more the tails, the more powerful they are.

Naruto was placed in a team of 3. Naruto Sakura Sasuke. Sasuke was a genius, Naruto was a dumb ninja. Naruto admired Sasuke so much. fast forward..... Sasuke left the village, and Naruto left his comfort zone to go find his friend. On finding him, Sasuke engaged him in a battle. He still didn't follow baba home, so waste of time jare. Protector

Naruto finally discovered his father was the previous Hokage (ninja governor). He became the protector the hidden leaf village, you don't wana fight that guy , trust me.
